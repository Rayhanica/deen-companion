# Ecosystem Delivery Roadmap

## Release 1 — Foundation

- Intent-based navigation and global search.
- Relational Supabase migration.
- Verified source and scholar-review workflow.
- Learning paths and community foundation.
- Personal vault and family guest profiles.
- Full-text and hybrid-search contracts.
- AI confidence and scholarly-difference metadata.
- Basic service-worker caching and offline manifests.

## Release 2 — Local Knowledge Corpus

- Quran import, multiple licensed translations, words, roots, and morphology.
- Hadith collection ingestion with canonical numbering.
- Tafsir link and licensing program.
- Article CMS, review queues, version history, and localization.
- Search analytics and relevance evaluation.

## Release 3 — Education and Hifz

- Lesson player, quizzes, exams, certificates.
- Spaced repetition scheduler and memorization heat maps.
- Audio submissions, waveform comments, teacher portal.
- Quran vocabulary flashcards and root explorer.
- Book library, highlights, notes, and chapter quizzes.

## Release 4 — Family and Community

- Household invitations and parental permissions.
- Child learning assignments and family goals.
- Verified masjid administration, events, khutbahs, and announcements.
- Study circles, volunteer workflows, teacher profiles, and follows.

## Release 5 — Native and Offline

- Expo iOS and Android applications.
- SQLite-backed encrypted offline library.
- Background content/audio downloads.
- Prayer widgets, lock-screen countdowns, and push notifications.
- Conflict-tested synchronization.

## Release 6 — Platform

- Mosque, school, academy, and scholar workspaces.
- Subscriptions, family plans, teacher plans, and organization billing.
- Multi-region reads, partitioned analytics, queues, observability, and trust-and-safety operations.

## Success metrics

- Prayer and Quran weekly active usage.
- Learning-path lesson completion.
- Hifz review adherence and weak-ayah recovery.
- Search success rate and citation opens.
- Percentage of published content with verified provenance.
- Scholar-review turnaround and correction rate.
- Family weekly participation.
- Community event and class conversion.
